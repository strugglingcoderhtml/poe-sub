Rooted 4 U is not only a service, it is a movement that inspires identity and awareness. Due to the rising demand for non-chemical based protective hairstyling, we recognise the decline in salons that have expertise with curly hair. We are your affordable and accessible hair care salon with a wide selection of protective curly hairstyles. We aim to educate our customers on maintaining healthy hair while providing excellent services.

Our Roots

Rooted 4 U Hair Studio exists within a social and economic context where many people of colour experience pressure to alter their natural hair texture. Many Black women continue to use chemical relaxers due to social expectations, convenience, and limited education surrounding natural hair care.

In South Africa, this trend is significant, with many women choosing to relax their hair because it is perceived as easier to manage. However, incorrect use of these chemicals can cause scalp irritation, hair breakage, and long-term damage.

Another contributing factor is the lack of accessible education about caring for natural Afro-textured hair. Many individuals do not have access to specialised knowledge or affordable professional services.

Rooted 4 U addresses these challenges by providing affordable, education-focused services that promote confidence, healthy hair, and self-expression.

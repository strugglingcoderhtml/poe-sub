Hair Services

  • Wash and Detangling
    A gentle cleansing and detangling process designed specifically for curly and coily hair. This service removes product buildup while restoring moisture balance. We carefully detangle using the right tools and techniques to prevent breakage, reduce shedding, and prepare your hair for styling in a healthy and manageable state.
  • Protective Styling
    Protective styling helps shield your natural hair from daily manipulation, environmental stress, and damage. This service includes styles that tuck away your ends, promote length retention, and maintain moisture. It is ideal for maintaining healthy hair while still enjoying versatile, stylish looks that last longer with minimal daily maintenance.
  • Braids and Twists
    Our braids and twists are carefully installed to ensure neatness, comfort, and long-lasting results. These styles support hair growth by reducing tension and breakage while offering a low-maintenance routine. Each look is customised to suit your hair type, length, and personal style, giving you both beauty and practicality.
  • Natural Hair Masks and Treatments
    This service focuses on restoring your hair’s strength and moisture through deep conditioning and natural treatments. We use nourishing ingredients and botanical oils to repair damage, improve elasticity, and enhance shine. Regular treatments help revive dry or brittle hair, leaving it softer, healthier, and more resilient over time.
  • Hair Trimming
    Regular trimming is essential for maintaining healthy hair and preventing split ends from worsening. This service involves carefully cutting damaged ends while preserving your hair’s length and shape. It promotes stronger growth, reduces breakage, and keeps your hair looking full, neat, and well-maintained with a clean, refreshed appearance.
